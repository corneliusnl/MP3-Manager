Welcome to MP3SQL page


This is in no way an fancy page, it only does what it shall and nothing more!

MP3SQL is an product that takes a look at your MP3s and extract the tags into an textfile with the apropiate SQL commands. It also includes the size of the file.

The current version is v1.0.0.1 and is working for me
